About me

After a few years of taking photos as a pass-time, through many hours of training from my brother who has 12 years of professional photography experience, I decided to follow in his footsteps and make a career of it.

I'm an outgoing person, and nothing brings me more joy than capturing moments that you and your family can remember for years to come.
